隐私政策

生效日期：2026年3月22日

OmniCalc（以下简称"本应用"）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了本应用如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

信息收集

本应用是一款本地计算器工具，我们的核心原则是最小化数据收集：

  • 计算数据：您的计算记录和历史仅存储在您的设备本地，不会上传至任何服务器。
  • 设置偏好：您的主题、语言等设置偏好仅存储在设备本地。
  • 不收集个人信息：本应用不收集您的姓名、邮箱、电话号码或其他个人身份信息。

数据存储

所有数据均存储在您的 iOS 设备本地，使用 Apple 的 SwiftData 框架进行安全存储。我们不运营任何用于存储用户数据的外部服务器。

第三方服务

本应用不使用任何第三方分析工具、广告 SDK 或追踪服务。

数据安全

您的数据受到 iOS 系统级别的安全保护，包括应用沙箱机制和设备加密。

儿童隐私

本应用不针对13岁以下的儿童，也不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新后的政策将在本页面发布，并更新生效日期。

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 22, 2026

OmniCalc ("the App") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information Collection

The App is a local calculator tool. Our core principle is to minimize data collection:

  • Calculation Data: Your calculations and history are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to any server.
  • Preferences: Your theme, language, and other preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No Personal Information: The App does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate any external servers for storing user data.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS system-level security, including app sandboxing and device encryption.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
